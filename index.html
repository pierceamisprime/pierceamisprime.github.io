About

Hello! I am a extremely motivated software engineer with prominent skills not limited to python, javascript, react, redux, sequelize, AWS, and more. Coming from a background mostly in the service industry, i've learned to take my critical thinking skills, and positive attitude and apply it to my software engineering journey. I'm relentless at trying to master my craft and truly optimize my skills. I've developed a lot of skill by collaborating with others and finding ways to maximize the teams potential. I'm an avid listener and observer and use critical thinking to make the appropriate adjustments to better achieve the teams goals. I would love to join your team and contribute in any positive way I can. I find there is something peaceful about software engineering and often feel blissful while dealing with broken code. I love the hunt for bugs and the rewarding feeling you get when you find and fix them. Its an amazing feeling when you can use all these different skills to solve problems, and what makes it better is it's ever evolving. There is always different solutions to different problems that require a totally different set of skills, and I know I will always be there adding those skills to my tool belt.
